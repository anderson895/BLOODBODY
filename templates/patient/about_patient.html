ABOUT BLOODBUDDY

BloodBuddy is a website developed by Medical Technology students from National University - Mall of Asia. This was developed to help users determine their blood type and compatibility for blood donation or transfusion.

Specifically, it can guide blood donors to find suitable recipients. In addition, it can also help those who need blood to find compatible blood donors.
